Employee onboarding begins with an orientation session covering company policy.

All new hires must complete security awareness training within the first week.

Expense reports must be submitted through the finance portal by the fifth business day of each month.

Remote employees are provided a stipend for home office equipment.

The performance review cycle runs twice per year, in April and October.

Confidential HR records are restricted to managers and the People Operations team.

Payroll is processed on the last business day of every month.
